Facts & Frequently Asked Questions

What is Buddhism?

Buddhism teaches the ultimate Law underlying life and the universe. In Nichiren Shoshu, we reveal this Law—Myoho-Renge-Kyo—through practice.

Who is Nichiren Daishonin?

The founder of Nichiren Shoshu Buddhism in 13th-century Japan, who revealed the means for people in this age to attain Buddhahood.

What is Karma?

Karma means action and its effects. Through correct practice, we transform negative causes and create benefit for ourselves and others.

What is “benefit”?

Visible and invisible positive effects from faith and practice—ranging from inner stability to improved circumstances.

What are the Three Treasures?

The Buddha, the Law, and the priesthood—protected and transmitted in Nichiren Shoshu temples worldwide.
