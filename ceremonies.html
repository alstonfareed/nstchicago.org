Ceremonies

Nichiren Shoshu temples conduct Buddhist ceremonies that support practice through life’s milestones—guided by the priesthood and the Law.

Gongyo & Shodai

Daily service (Gongyo) and chanting practice (Shodai). Visitors are welcome to observe and learn.

Memorial Services

Prayers for ancestors and loved ones, conducted according to Nichiren Shoshu teachings.

Seasonal observances and significant anniversaries held throughout the year.
