What is the Purpose of Faith?

Faith in Nichiren Shoshu is not blind belief—it is a deep trust in Myoho-Renge-Kyo, which reveals our inherent Buddha nature.

This faith empowers us to lead a life of courage, clarity, and wisdom. It enables us to rely on the Mystic Law even in adversity and fosters the conviction that our practice is effective.
