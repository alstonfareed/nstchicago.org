What is Benefit?

When you practice Nichiren Shoshu Buddhism—chanting Nam-Myoho-Renge-Kyo and doing Gongyo—you receive benefit in two ways.

  • Conspicuous benefit: Results that are immediate and clearly noticeable.
  • Inconspicuous benefit: Changes that happen over time—deeper shifts in life that may not be obvious day to day but are undeniable when you look back.

These benefits affirm that the Law is alive and that our practice works. They encourage us to advance joyfully for ourselves and others.
