Temples & Community

Nichiren Shoshu has temples worldwide, including the Head Temple Taisekiji in Japan and local temples across North America. Myogyoji serves the Midwest.

Head Temple Taisekiji

The Head Temple is located at the foot of Mt. Fuji in Japan. Pilgrimage (Tozan) is a profound opportunity to deepen faith.
